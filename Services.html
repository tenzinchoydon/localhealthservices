Mental Health Services


These focus on emotional, psychological, and behavioral well-being. Common Services Include:

1.Therapy/Counseling (individual, group, family)

2.Psychiatric services (diagnosis, medication management)

3.Crisis intervention (hotlines, emergency support)

4.Substance abuse treatment

5.Community mental health programs

6.Support groups (e.g., for anxiety, depression, grief)

7.Inpatient/outpatient psychiatric care


Physical Health Services

These focus on diagnosing, treating, and preventing physical illness or injury. Common Services Include:
1.Primary care (routine checkups, general health concerns)

2.Specialist care (cardiology, dermatology, etc.)

3.Emergency services (ER, urgent care)

4.Preventive care (vaccinations, screenings)

5.Rehabilitation services (physical therapy, occupational therapy)

6.Surgical services

7.Chronic disease management (e.g., diabetes, hypertension)
